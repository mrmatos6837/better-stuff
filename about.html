We believe makers deserve better tools

Better Stuff started with a simple observation: the people who build things for a living are often stuck using mediocre tools and outdated workflows. They deserve better.

We're building a platform that gives makers — developers, designers, founders, creators — the tools, insights, and community they need to ship higher-quality work, faster.

The problem we're solving

Most product teams waste time on three things: context-switching between disconnected tools, guessing what users actually want, and doing repetitive work that should be automated. The result is slower shipping, lower quality, and burnout.

Better Stuff brings together the workflows, metrics, and community feedback that makers need — in one place, without the bloat.

Our values

Quality over quantity

We'd rather ship one thing that works perfectly than ten things that sort of work. We hold ourselves to the same standard we set for our users.

Speed through simplicity

Complexity is the enemy of shipping. We remove friction, automate the mundane, and keep interfaces clean so you can focus on the work that matters.

Community first

The best products are built with real feedback from real people. We build in the open and listen to our community at every step.

Where we're headed

We're in the early stages — building the foundation, gathering feedback, and growing our community of early adopters. Our roadmap is shaped by what makers tell us they need most.

If you care about shipping better work, we'd love to have you along for the ride.
